Privacy Policy

Here at ‘The Reunion App’ we take privacy and security very seriously. We are very committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. By accessing or using our services or app, you acknowledge that you have read, understood and agree to our collection, storage, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this policy. Please familiarize yourself with the following terms before reading this privacy statement: (1) The ‘Organizer’ – the organizer is the individual who is organizing or hosting the event. The organizer is the individual responsible for posting the event information on ‘The Reunion App’. (2) The ‘Participant’ – the participant is the individual(s) that is attending the event. (3) The term ‘we’ refers to the developers and owners of ‘The Reunion App’.

Participant

‘The Reunion App’ does not require any personal information as such as a profile name etc. It does however identify your account with a unique user number. This number and account can be deleted at any time using the 'Delete Account' button on the profile page.

Organizer

The Organizer will be asked to provide information only if they agree to purchase a Process Payment Plan for their event. The Process Payment Plan allows the participants to pay for registration or items such as T-shirts through the app. However, the Organizer should provide the following information: first name, last name, and email address. This information is needed if there are issues with the process payment request. All other information such as the street address, city, state, zip, and phone number is not required but recommended. All payment or credit card information is handled by Square and is not seen by ‘The Reunion App’. If you wish to delete your account, you can do so by using the ‘Delete Account’ button on your profile page.
