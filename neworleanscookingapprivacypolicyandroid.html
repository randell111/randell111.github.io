Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how New Orleans Cooking App (“we,” “us,” or “our”) handles your information when you use our mobile application (New Orleans Cooking App).

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We are committed to protecting your privacy. New Orleans Cooking App currently is designed with a fundamental principle: we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or personally identifiable information from our users.

This means:

• We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other identifying information.

• We do not access or store your contacts, photos, or any other data from your device.

• We do not use analytics tools that track your individual usage patterns.

2. Third-Party Transaction Processing (Square APIs)

While we do not collect your data, our App facilitates transactions using Square APIs. When you make a purchase or complete a transaction through our App, your payment information (such as credit card details) is processed directly by Square. We do not store or have access to your sensitive financial data. For information on how Square collects, uses, and protects your data, please refer to Square’s Privacy Policy. You can typically find this on Square’s official website or during the transaction process.

3. No Use of Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use your information for any purpose, including but not limited to:

• Advertising or marketing

• Selling or sharing with third parties

• Personalization or profiling

4. Data Security

Because we do not collect or store any user data, there is no personal data for us to secure. Your direct interactions with Square for transaction processing are subject to Square’s security measures.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not directed to, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from, children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently received personal information from a child under 13, we will delete such information from our records.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at neworleanscookingapp@gmail.com.
